  • ice cream

    Ice cream contains six major components: water, fat, protein, sugar, stabilizers, and emulsifiers. Typically, milk and cream provide the water, fat, and proteins.

    To meet the U.S. Food & Drug Administration standard definition of ice cream, a product must contain at least 10% milk fat by weight.

  • ice coffee

    Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee normally and serving it over ice or in cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold.

    In hot brewing, sweeteners and flavoring may be added before cooling, as they dissolve faster.
