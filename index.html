Franchise agreement

Most ICECREAM restaurants are owned by independent operators who enter into a franchise agreement with the company. Franchisees must have a business background and the capital required to open a restaurant. “Entrepreneur” listed ICECREAM restaurant startup costs from $500000 to $100000. Franchise agreements are for 20 years and include a number of standards and legal requirements that franchisees must follow, including adhering to menu items, employee training, and the use of company products, such as soft goods. ICECREAM has terminated franchise agreements for a variety of infractions.
